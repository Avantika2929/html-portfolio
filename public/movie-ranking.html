The Best Movies According to AVANTIKA

My Top 3 Movies of all time.

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

a reserved boy who discovers his cheerful classmate Sakura’s secret—she has a terminal illness. Their growing bond changes his life as they cherish fleeting moments together.

Spirited Away

Chihiro, a young girl trapped in a mystical world ruled by spirits. To save her parents and return home, she must work at a magical bathhouse.

My Neighbor Totoro

sisters Satsuki and Mei, who move to the countryside and befriend Totoro, a gentle forest spirit. Together, they embark on magical adventures while awaiting their mother's recovery
